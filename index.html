About the business:

Cindy's is a family-owned, American diner with a retro look and feel that has been serving the community of Eagle Rock and the overall northeast Los Angeles for over 70 years. We serve a classic American menu for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. And on top of the great food, customer service, and ambiance, Cindy's has also been featured in numerous film, TV, and music productions.
